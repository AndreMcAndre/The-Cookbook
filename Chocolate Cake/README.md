## Ingredients
### The cake
* 100g haslenuts
* 200g marzipan
* 200g sugar
* 200g butter
* 4 eggs
* 75g dark chocolate
* 75g milk chocolate

### The glaze
* Cinnamon
* Coaca powder
* Powderd sugar
* Tap water
* 25g white or dark chocolate (Optional)
