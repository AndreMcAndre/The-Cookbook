Preparation: 15mins

## Ingredients
* 200g flour T65
* 50g cornstarch
* 2 eggs
* 50cl milk
* 1 pinch of salt
* 50g melted butter
## Materials
* A big bowl
* A whisk
* A pan
## Method
1. Add every ingredients in the bowl and mix.
